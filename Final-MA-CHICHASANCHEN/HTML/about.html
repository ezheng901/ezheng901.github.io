Chi Cha San Chen: Luxury Tea

Chi Cha San Chen is a Taiwan-founded tea company. Starting in 1998, we were especially known for our fresh ingrediants and warm atmosphere. However, the most notable difference between us and other tea shops is that we freshly brew our tea every for every single portion, and the quality of the tea is unlike any other. Tight regulations and restrictions insure the utmost quality coming from our ingredients.

Quality

We pride ourselves in the quality of every cup of tea we put out. Heavy restrictions and regulations ensure that each beverage is the exact same from the first to the second, third, fourth, or nth time you come! However, nothing comes close to the importance of our tea leaves, where they are grown all over Taiwan!
